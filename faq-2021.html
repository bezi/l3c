  • I'm having issues with the Cisco SG350 interfacing with Cisco IE2000 switches. Do you have any advice on working with industrial network switches?
  • Generally we've found that SFP Compatibility between Cisco Small Business switches (such as the SG350) and the Cisco Industrial Switches (like the IE2000) is poorly defined and can be finicky. For best results, stick to one class of switches or the other. Anecdotally, we've been happy with a combined fleet of IE2000s and IE4000s.
